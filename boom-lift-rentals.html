Selecting the right boom

Different boom lift configurations for different site conditions.

We look at your height, outreach, access path and ground surface before suggesting articulated or telescopic booms, so the machine fits the job instead of forcing the job to fit the machine.

Articulated boom lifts are helpful when you have to rise up and then move over an obstruction ‚Äì such as canopies, ducts or projecting structures. They give flexibility where the work point is not directly above the base of the machine.

Telescopic booms are used when long, straight reach is more important ‚Äì for example, when working along the side of a tall industrial building, pipe rack or bridge. These machines are often deployed on large, open sites with clear travel paths.

If you share the approximate working height, distance from the work area and a note on the ground surface, our team can recommend which boom style and size will be realistic for your project.
