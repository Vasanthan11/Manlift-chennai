Deep dive: choosing the right lift for façade works

Façade projects often require a balance between working height, outreach and the need to move along the face. Articulated booms provide the ability to negotiate obstructions while telescopic booms give long, straight reach when the ground allows.

When planning, share the working height, horizontal distance from a practical stand position and whether the job is indoors or outdoors. Photos or a sketch help us confirm whether an articulated or telescopic boom will reduce repositioning time and overall rental cost.
